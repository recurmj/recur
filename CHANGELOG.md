- **Recur Whitepaper v1.1** PDF:
  - Added Reader’s Note explaining the relationship between the whitepaper,
    *The Geometry of Consent*, and *The Discovery of the Consent Constant*.

## [1.0] — 2025-10-17
### Added
- Initial publication of **Recur Whitepaper v1.0**.
- Initial publication of **Recur Technical Paper v1.0**.

- Introduced core concepts: continuous liquidity, revocable consent, and proactive
  balance flow.
- Established the architectural and economic foundations of the Flow Layer.
- Added first reference contracts and documentation for the Recur standard.

### Notes

- First public publication on recurprotocol.com and in this GitHub repository.
